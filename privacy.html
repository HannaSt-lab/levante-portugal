Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Who we are

LEVANTE is a private tour operator based in Lisbon, Portugal. We design bespoke Portugal travel experiences for a small number of clients each year.

Data controller: Hanna Stetsovich, operating as LEVANTE, Lisbon, Portugal.
Contact: hannastetsovich@gmail.com

Data we collect

We collect personal data only when you choose to contact us or interact with our platforms:

  • Contact form — name, email address, country, and the message you send
  • Calendly bookings — name, email address, and appointment time
  • Instagram direct messages — your Instagram user ID and the text of messages you send to our account
  • Facebook Lead Ads — name and email address submitted via Meta advertising forms

We do not collect payment information directly. Deposits and payments are processed by Stripe, which operates under its own privacy policy.

How we use your data

We use the data we collect solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiry and design a travel experience for you
  • Send you a confirmation when you book a consultation call
  • Send an automated reply when you message us on Instagram
  • Follow up if you contacted us and we have not yet spoken
  • Send you our newsletter if you have subscribed

We do not sell your data. We do not use it for advertising. We do not share it with third parties except as described below.

Third parties

To operate our business, we use the following services, each of which may process your data:

  • GoHighLevel — CRM platform where we store contact records
  • n8n — automation platform that connects our tools
  • Calendly — consultation booking platform
  • Google (Gmail) — email delivery
  • Meta (Facebook / Instagram) — messaging and lead forms
  • Slack — internal team notifications
  • Stripe — payment processing

All third-party processors are contractually bound to handle data lawfully and in accordance with applicable data protection regulations.

Legal basis

We process your data on the following legal bases under the GDPR:

  • Contractual necessity — to respond to your inquiry and deliver a service you have requested
  • Legitimate interest — to follow up on inquiries and send the review request after your tour
  • Consent — for newsletter subscriptions, which you may withdraw at any time

Data retention

We retain your contact details for as long as necessary to deliver your experience and for a reasonable period afterward in case you travel with us again. You may request deletion at any time.

Instagram message data is retained only for the purpose of the automated reply and is not stored beyond that interaction.

Your rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Receive your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below. We will respond within 30 days.
