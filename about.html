The curator

I know what
Portugal misses.

LEVANTE exists in the gap between the visible version of a country and the one you have to be invited into.

Founded by
Hanna
Founder · Curator

I grew up between Lisbon and London. Between two cities that each think they invented cool, each convinced the other one doesn't understand it.

That in-between position teaches you something: every place has two versions. The one in the guide, and the one the guide can't reach.

I spent years inside both Lisbons. Working with the people who made the city rather than the ones who described it. Knowing which wine list was put together by someone who cared, and which was put together by someone who googled. Learning which introductions took years to earn, and which you simply can't buy.

LEVANTE started because people kept asking me to share it. Not clients at first — friends, then friends of friends, then people who found me some other way. The question was always the same: I'm going to Portugal. What do you actually know?

Now I build itineraries for a small number of people each year. No algorithm, no template. Three weeks of work before you arrive. The kind of preparation that means when you sit down to dinner in a room of nine people, you're there because we thought about who you are.

How we work

The things we won't compromise on.

01
One party at a time.

Each journey is designed for one group, not adapted from a menu. We take on a limited number of guests per year so that this stays true.

02
No commissions. No hotel deals.

We recommend what we would stay in ourselves. Hotels and restaurants earn their place by being right, not by having a relationship with us.

03
The access is real.

When we say a private workshop, a closed vineyard, a kitchen table — it means we've sat there. The introductions are ours to make because we made them first.
