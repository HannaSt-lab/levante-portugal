01 — Our Story

Born between
three countries.

LEVANTE was founded by Hanna. Growing up between three countries gave her an early understanding that every place has two versions — the one most people see, and the one you have to be invited into. Portugal, she found, has the widest gap of any of them.

Every LEVANTE experience begins with a conversation and ends with something no platform can replicate. Not because of what we add — because of what we know to remove.

03 — Philosophy

The places worth visiting
don't advertise themselves.

Most travel experiences are built on the same information: the same review platforms, the same "hidden gems" that aren't. LEVANTE works differently. Our network is built over years of living here — the kind of relationships that get you into the private cellar, the after-hours studio, the courtyard with no sign.

We don't offer packages. We don't have a catalogue. What we offer is access — to a version of Portugal that doesn't exist in any brochure.

Portugal rewards the patient and the specific. We do the patience on your behalf.
