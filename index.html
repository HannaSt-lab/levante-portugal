Hanna — Founder

Born between
three countries.

Growing up between three countries gave me an early understanding that every place has two versions — the one most people see, and the one you have to be invited into. Portugal, I found, has the widest gap of any of them.

Every LEVANTE journey begins with a conversation and ends with something no platform can replicate — not because of what we add, but because of what we know to remove.

I grew up knowing there's the visible version of a place — and the one you have to be invited into. LEVANTE is about the second.
